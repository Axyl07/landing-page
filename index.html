Infinity Stones

The Infinity Stones are six powerful gems in the Marvel Universe: Space, Time, Reality, Power, Mind, and Soul. Each grants unique abilities, and when combined, they provide unlimited power. They are central to many Marvel storylines, especially involving Thanos.
